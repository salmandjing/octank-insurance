# Octank Insurance - Claims Procedures

## How to File a Claim

### Step 1: Report the Incident
Contact Octank Insurance as soon as possible after an incident. You can file a claim through:
- **Online**: Log in to your Octank member portal at octankinsurance.com
- **Phone**: Call our 24/7 claims line at 1-800-OCTANK-1
- **Virtual Agent**: Use our virtual assistant for guided claim filing
- **Mobile App**: File directly from the Octank Insurance app

### Step 2: Provide Required Information
When filing a claim, please have the following ready:
- Your policy number and member ID
- Date, time, and location of the incident
- Description of what happened
- Names and contact information of all parties involved
- Police report number (if applicable)
- Photos of the damage (if available)
- Witness information (if any)

### Step 3: Adjuster Assignment
- A claims adjuster will be assigned within **24 hours** of filing
- The adjuster will contact you to schedule a vehicle inspection
- Inspections are typically completed within **3-5 business days**

### Step 4: Damage Assessment
- The adjuster will evaluate the damage and provide an estimate
- If repairs exceed the vehicle's actual cash value, it may be declared a total loss
- You will receive the assessment in writing

### Step 5: Claim Resolution
- **Approved claims**: Repair authorization issued or payment sent
- **Denied claims**: You will receive a written explanation with appeal instructions
- **Typical timeline**: 15-30 business days from filing to resolution

## Claim Status Definitions

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Filed** | Claim received and being processed |
| **Under Review** | Adjuster assigned and investigation in progress |
| **Inspection Scheduled** | Vehicle inspection has been scheduled |
| **Assessment Complete** | Damage has been evaluated and estimate prepared |
| **Approved** | Claim approved for payment or repair |
| **Payment Issued** | Payment has been sent to the member or repair facility |
| **Denied** | Claim has been denied (see denial letter for details) |
| **Closed** | Claim has been fully resolved |

## Required Documentation by Claim Type

### Collision Claims
- Police report (required if another driver involved)
- Photos of damage to all vehicles
- Other driver's insurance information
- Witness statements

### Comprehensive Claims (Theft)
- Police report (required)
- List of personal items in the vehicle
- Copy of vehicle title
- Last known location and time of theft

### Comprehensive Claims (Weather/Natural Disaster)
- Photos of damage
- Date and description of weather event
- Verification from National Weather Service (for disputed events)

### Comprehensive Claims (Animal Strike)
- Photos of damage
- Location of incident
- Police report (recommended but not required)

## Appeals Process
If your claim is denied, you have **60 days** to file an appeal:
1. Review the denial letter for the specific reason
2. Gather additional documentation supporting your claim
3. Submit a written appeal to claims@octankinsurance.com
4. Appeals are reviewed within 15 business days
5. You will receive a written decision
