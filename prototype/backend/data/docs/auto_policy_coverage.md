# Octank Insurance - Auto Policy Coverage Guide

## Coverage Types

### Liability Coverage
Liability coverage is required by law in most states and pays for injuries and property damage you cause to others in an accident.
- **Bodily Injury Liability**: Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and legal fees for people you injure in an accident. Octank offers limits from $25,000/$50,000 to $500,000/$1,000,000.
- **Property Damage Liability**: Covers damage you cause to another person's property. Limits range from $25,000 to $500,000.

### Collision Coverage
Collision coverage pays to repair or replace your vehicle after a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of who is at fault.
- Subject to your chosen deductible ($250, $500, or $1,000)
- Covers the actual cash value of your vehicle minus the deductible
- Required if you have an auto loan or lease

### Comprehensive Coverage
Comprehensive coverage protects your vehicle from non-collision damage including:
- Theft and vandalism
- Hail, flood, and weather damage
- Falling objects and debris
- Animal collisions (e.g., deer strikes)
- Fire damage
- Windshield and glass damage
Subject to your chosen deductible.

### Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage
Protects you if you're hit by a driver with no insurance or insufficient coverage.
- Covers medical bills and lost wages
- Available in bodily injury and property damage variants
- Highly recommended by Octank Insurance

### Additional Coverages
- **Rental Car Reimbursement**: Up to $50/day for 30 days while your car is being repaired after a covered claim
- **Roadside Assistance**: 24/7 towing, flat tire, lockout, and fuel delivery service
- **Gap Coverage**: Pays the difference between your car's value and what you owe on your loan

## Exclusions

The following are NOT covered under standard Octank auto policies:
- Intentional damage to your own vehicle
- Damage while using your vehicle for commercial purposes (rideshare, delivery) unless you have a commercial rider
- Mechanical breakdown or normal wear and tear
- Damage from racing or off-road use
- Personal belongings inside the vehicle (covered under homeowner's/renter's insurance)
- Damage while driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol

## Filing a Claim

If you need to file a claim:
1. Ensure everyone is safe and call 911 if needed
2. Document the scene with photos
3. Exchange information with other parties
4. File a police report if applicable
5. Contact Octank Insurance within 72 hours
6. An adjuster will be assigned within 24 hours

For emergencies, call our 24/7 claims hotline: 1-800-OCTANK-1
